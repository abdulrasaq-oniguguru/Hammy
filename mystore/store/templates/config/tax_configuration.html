Quick Reference
Inclusive Tax:

Tax is already included in the product price. The system calculates the tax portion from the total.

Example: Product costs ₦10,750 including 7.5% VAT.
Tax Amount = ₦10,750 - (₦10,750 / 1.075) = ₦750
Price without tax = ₦10,000

Exclusive Tax:

Tax is added on top of the product price.

Example: Product costs ₦10,000 + 7.5% VAT.
Tax Amount = ₦10,000 × 0.075 = ₦750
Total Price = ₦10,750
